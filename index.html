Better health, together

CareClique empowers communities to manage chronic diseases by addressing social determinants of health.

How It Works

1. Complete Initial Screening

We use research-based tools to best screen for social determinants of health.

2. Receive Recommendedations

You receive customized reccomendations on health & wellness goals and services based on factors relevant to you, like your location and health history.

3. Set And Track Goals

Create a plan that works! We'll give you some recommendations, but our flexible platform also enables creating custom goals that make sense for you.

4. Add Your Clique

We provide tools that enable your family, friends, and health stakeholders to support your progress.

5. Access Key Resources

We believe everybody should be able to access the health and social resources they need. CareClique makes this possible by empowering those around you to help meet your goals.
