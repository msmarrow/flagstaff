Meet Our Team

We founded CareClique because we believe in the power of community. Recognizing inequalities in access to healthcare and social services, we aim to ameliorate disparities by creating software that empowers users to develop health and wellness strategies that leverage the collective resources of both our platform and their personal networks. To do this, we bring together our backgrounds in medicine, public health research, technology, and ethics to develop a person-first tool for our users.
